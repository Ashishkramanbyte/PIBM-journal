    PIBM welcomes all the esteemed authors to A Journal of Management, an esteemed platform dedicated to showcasing and celebrating invaluable contributions to the field of management. This platform serves as a nurturing space where researchers, scholars, and practitioners can share their innovative work and insights.

    We invite authors from various management disciplines to submit their original research, empirical studies, case analyses, literature reviews, and conceptual articles. Our rigorous double-blind peer review process ensures that all submissions receive thorough evaluation from experts, maintaining the highest standards of quality and credibility.

    For those eager to share their research with the world, we welcome you to register as a new author and submit your manuscript following our comprehensive "Guide for Authors" section.

    Registered authors can enjoy a seamless experience by signing in to check the status of their submitted manuscripts, access peer review comments, and monitor the progress of their articles through our user-friendly platform.

    Join us in enriching the realm of management knowledge and making a lasting impact on the field.
