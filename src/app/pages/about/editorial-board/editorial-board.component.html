    Meet the esteemed Editorial Board of PIBM’s A Journal of Management, a dynamic and visionary team committed to steering the scholarly course of our publication. As the official Management Journal of the Pune Institute of Business Management (PIBM), our Editorial Board plays a central role in upholding the journal's academic integrity and excellence.

    Comprising distinguished scholars and seasoned practitioners from diverse management disciplines, our board members meticulously reviews submitted manuscripts. Their expertise ensures that only the most impactful and innovative research graces our pages, empowering readers with cutting-edge insights.

    Each member brings a unique perspective, fostering an inclusive environment that encourages intellectual rigor and open discourse. Their guidance drives our pursuit of excellence, empowering researchers and readers to shape the future of management research.
